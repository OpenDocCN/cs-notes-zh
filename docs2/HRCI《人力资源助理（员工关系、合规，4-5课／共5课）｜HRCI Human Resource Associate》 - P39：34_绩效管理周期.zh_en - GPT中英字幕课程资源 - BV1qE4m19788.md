In this video， we will explore the key phases involved in effectively managing employee performance in every organization performance management functions as an ongoing cycle。

 this continuous four step cycle ensures employee performance is effectively monitored。

 evaluated and improved over time， enabling organizations to achieve their goals。😊。



![](img/7a84ddd8be2ad2b07a98b89d122aea0b_2.png)

It is important to note that the specific timing for measuring。

 evaluating and providing feedback may vary from one organization to another let's discuss each phase using the marketing manager and a graphic designer as examples at the beginning of the performance cycle the marketing manager defines performance expectations for the evaluation period。

 which is typically one year they discuss specific design projects， deadlines。

 quality standards and other relevant performance criteria with the graphic designer。😊。

This initial communication sets the foundation for a shared understanding of what the organization expects from the employee regarding goals。

 targets， and performance standards。During the evaluation phase。

 the manager closely monitors the designer's performance， such as their creativity， technical skills。

 adherence to brand guidelines and ability to meet project goals。

 the manager uses various assessment methods， including regular checkins。

 performance reviews and performance metrics to assess the designer's progress。

 accomplishments and areas for improvement。In the next phase。

 the manager uses the evaluation results to give the designer constructive feedback。

They address the designer's attention to detail how well they respond to client requests and their collaboration with other team members。

 the manager highlights their creativity and time management skills and offers ways for them to collaborate more effectively with the team and stay up to date with the latest industry trends。

Constructive feedback helps the designer understand their performance gaps and take appropriate actions to improve their skills。

 knowledge and effectiveness。After their performance review and feedback session。

 the graphic designer adjust their approach based on a feedback they received。

 they maintain regular collaboration with team members and find online sources to stay updated with design related news building on this progress。

 the manager and the designer discuss and set performance expectations for the next performance cycle these expectations include new design projects。

 objectives， potential skill development opportunities and aligning their goals with the organization。

😊。
