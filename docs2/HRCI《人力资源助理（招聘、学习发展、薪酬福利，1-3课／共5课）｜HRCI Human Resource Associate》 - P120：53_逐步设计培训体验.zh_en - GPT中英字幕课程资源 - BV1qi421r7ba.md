![](img/73767990e3e9b35c9b0f7a83d3eee840_2.png)

Conduct training needs analysis， create a smart goal。Identify how to measure success。

 Select a training delivery method， select a training type and design a post training evaluation。

 Ur attire is creating a sexual harassment compliance training。

 The human resources team wants to make sure the training is impactful and relevant to each role To do this。

 they conduct a training needs analysis with the different teams in the organization to identify areas where their employees need to improve or identify gaps in the current training。



![](img/73767990e3e9b35c9b0f7a83d3eee840_4.png)

The needs analysis shows a need for sexual harassment training in the following areas。

 What constitutes sexual harassment and how to prevent and report incidents。

 The next step is for urban attire to set a smart goal so that there is a way to measure the implementation of the training across the organization。

 After a meeting as a department， It is decided that the smart goal is by the end of the compliance training program。

90% of the employees will be able to identify and respond appropriately to sexual harassment and the number of sexual harassment incident in a quarterly period is lowered by 10%。

To measure the success of the compliance program for individual employees。

 Urban attire will conduct a pre training and post training assessment。

 and they will compare the results。In the assessments， scenarios will be presented。

 employees must identify if it is a case of sexual harassment。

 and then they must choose in appropriate response to the scenario。

Urban attire decides to use an onsite classroom based delivery for training onsite classroom training lowers the cost of the training by eliminating the need for travel。

 in addition it will allow for direct feedback from trainers on real world scenarios involving sexual harassment。

Having immediate feedback is critical for the Human Res Department to clear any misconceptions before employees leave the training。

Next， Urban attire determines the training type the department decides on an instructor led training program that includes interactive exercises。

 role playing scenarios and group discussions This type of training will enable employees to participate and practice real life scenarios in a safe environment  employees will also get feedback on identifying instances of sexual harassment and how to respond。



![](img/73767990e3e9b35c9b0f7a83d3eee840_6.png)

Le， an evaluation for the training needs to be designed。Discussions within the department。

 interviews with employees who attended the training。

 focus group discussions and feedback forms will be used to evaluate the training the training evaluation results will also be presented to organization leadership and adjustments will be made to future sexual harassment training based on the data gathered from this training evaluation。
