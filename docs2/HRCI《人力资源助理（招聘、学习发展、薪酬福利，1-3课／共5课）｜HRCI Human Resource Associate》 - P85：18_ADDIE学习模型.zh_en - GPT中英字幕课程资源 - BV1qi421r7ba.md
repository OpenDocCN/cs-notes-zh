The ADDI Learn model is a flexible model used to create effective training that meets the specific needs of employees using AddI you consider the current skills of employees and tailor a program to fill the gaps。



![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_2.png)

Addie is a process for designing training programs that has five steps analysis， design， development。

 implementation and evaluation the analysis phase includes three steps the first is identifying the goal of the training program the second is gathering data on the employee's current skill levels or skill gaps the last step is to propose and evaluate a solution based on effectiveness。

 cost and time。

![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_4.png)

Imagine that you work in human resources at a fashion company named Urban Attire。

 You are tasked with preparing the sales team for an upcoming industry event。

 You want to train the team to implement a personalized sales approach to ensure that customers receive the best service。

The first step in the AddI model is to analyze the training needs of the sales team。

 this involves gathering data on the current skill levels of the team members as well as the knowledge and skills that they need to be successful in their roles。

You can use surveys， interviews， and observations to gather this data。



![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_6.png)

Your analysis reveals that the sales team members are not familiar with the company's new product line。

 They also lack the skills necessary to sell the new products to potential customers。

 The next phase design focuses on identifying the target audience。

 developing training objectives and creating content。

Let's go back to our scenario the next step is to design the training program。

 this involves creating a curriculum that meet the specific needs of the sales team your curriculum should be based on the data gathered during the analysis phase。



![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_8.png)

For the sales team training， the curriculum will cover the following topics。

 the features and benefits of the new product line， the sales process。

 and how to overcome objections。

![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_10.png)

In the third phase of Aie， we began development in this phase。

 instructional designers develop training materials， instructional methods and training methods。

 For example， will the training occur in a classroom online or through self study。

 Let's go through the development phase for urban attire。

 The training materials are developed in house by a team familiar with the new product line and the sales team methods used by the company。

 The materials include a variety of multimedia resources such as videos。

 interactive exercises and assessments。

![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_12.png)

![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_13.png)

During the next phase of implementation， instructional designers select a delivery method。

 hire and train instructors， and develop a training schedule。



![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_15.png)

Now let's explore how you can implement your sales training。

To lower the cost of training and allow the sales team to do some of it at their own pace。

 the training is delivered through a combination of classroom and online delivery methods。

The classroom sessions cover the core topics and the online training provides additional support and practice。

The final stage of ADI is evaluation， there are two types of evaluation。

 formative and summative formative evaluations are conducted throughout the ADDI process to inform design and development that include needs assessments。

 jobs analyses， pilot testing and preting。

![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_17.png)

![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_18.png)

Summative evaluations are conducted at the completion of training programs to assess their effectiveness and participant feedback。



![](img/6b13b470135d8154513b71c13475754a_20.png)

The sales team members evaluate the training program。

 a summative evaluation shows that the program was effective in meeting the needs of the sales team the team members are able to successfully sell new products to potential customers。

Using the Addie model， you developed an effective training program。

 your sales team has improved their product knowledge and sales skills they are now prepared for the upcoming event。
