 Our replacement chart can assist managers and HR professionals in succession planning。

 A replacement chart categorizes employees in four ways。 First。

 employees may be identified as ready for promotion。

 These are individuals who have the knowledge skills and abilities to take on greater responsibilities in the organization。

 Employ may also be identified as candidates to be developed for future promotion。

 These are promising employees who will be ready for more responsibilities after they complete more training or gain more experience。

😊，Next， employees may fall into the category of satisfactory in the current position。

 These are employees who perform their existing jobs proficiently。

 but do not demonstrate the skills or potential for promotion。Finally。

 employees may be identified as falling into the category replace。

 These are employees who are not expected to be part of the organization in the long term for one reason or another。

 The reasons may include retirement， transfer， promotion， disability leave or poor performance。
