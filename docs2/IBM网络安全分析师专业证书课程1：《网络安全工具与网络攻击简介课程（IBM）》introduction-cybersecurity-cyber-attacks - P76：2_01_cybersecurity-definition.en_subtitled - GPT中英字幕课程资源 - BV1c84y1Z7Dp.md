Information security。According to NIST， it is the protection of information systems from unauthorized activities in order to provide confidentiality。

 integrity and availability， these three principles are known as the CIA Triad。The CIA Triad。

Here we have three different principles， confidentiality， which is similar or equivalent to privacy。

For confidentiality， access to resources or data must be restricted to only authorized subjects or entities；

 data encryption is a common method of ensuring confidentiality。Integrity， on the other hand。

 involves maintaining the consistency and accuracy of data over its entire life cycle。

Data must not be changed in transit， for example， when it is sent over the internet or using a local area network。

And steps must be taken to ensure that no one or an authorized person or subject makes any changes to our data。
