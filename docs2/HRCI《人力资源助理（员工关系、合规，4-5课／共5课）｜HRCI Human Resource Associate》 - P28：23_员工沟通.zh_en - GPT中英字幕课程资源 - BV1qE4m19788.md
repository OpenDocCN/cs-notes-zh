Effective communication strategies are important to maintain a healthy workplace climate and ensure an organization runs effectively and efficiently In this video you'll learn about employee communicationEmploys often relay information to employees。

 especially to keep them informed about an organization's goals or policies however。

 employees should also feel they can communicate their thoughts and ideas to supervisors and managers and that those thoughts will be heard and taken seriously here are a few strategies for establishing effective communication within an organization。

The first strategy is an open door policy organizationss with open door policies encourage employees to meet with their supervisors or managers whenever an issue arises and without a formally scheduled meeting。

😊，Another strategy is management by walking around This communication strategy makes managers available by physically walking around the office during these walks。

 a manager might answer questions， check on how certain tasks are progressing and handle problems。

 although this management strategy might seem like an obvious communication step。

 it is easy for managers to forget to do it because of other demands。

 department meetings are another strategy。 department meetings typically occur at regularly scheduled intervals and include all staff within a department。

😊。

![](img/e6928bffc0a7f2c1724b4df0507b08f4_4.png)

These meetings are an opportunity to discuss relevant issues town hall meetings are formal meetings for all employees within an organization During these meetings most information is delivered by leadership to employees。

 although sometimes there are opportunities for two way communication。😊。

The final strategy is lunch and learn sessions These informal meetings allow employees to discuss various issues and concerns in a relaxed setting that includes both managers and employees。

😊，In addition to these strategies for establishing effective communication。

 there are several methods or delivery options that are commonly used。

 Each has both advantages and disadvantages。 Let's explore them now。😊。

The most common form of communication in most organizations is email email is an efficient way to communicate information to many people at once。

 and it also provides a cataloged paper trail， however。

 people can be overwhelmed with email communications and it's also possible for confidential communication to be accidentally shared and intratranet is another way for individuals within an organization to communicate and share information。

 however， individuals outside an organization are not able to access information placed on an intranet。

😊，The next communication method is a newsletter， a newsletter allows an organization to distribute information to employees about a variety of activities this communication is largely one sided however。

 and it can take a lot of time to prepare and distribute a newsletter。
