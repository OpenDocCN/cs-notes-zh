 but the concept of managing risk encompasses two categories。

 protecting the employee and protecting the organization。To successfully mitigate risks。

 it is crucial to understand your responsibilities as an HR professional。

Protecting employees involves three main components， safety and health programs。

 injury and illness prevention， and injury and accident response。



![](img/50751580efea2c9dd17e085c997973ff_2.png)

Safety and health programs vary depending on your field and organization。

 but most will focus on communicating policies and practices for workplace safety as well as training employees on those procedures。

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health or NIOSH， studies， trends。

 patternss and the science of workplaceplace Safety。Based on their research and analysis。

 Incorporating an injury and illness prevention program is also a way to protect employees。 In fact。

 an OSHA White Paper states its belief that injury and illness prevention programs provide the foundation for breakthrough changes and the way employers identify and control hazards leading to a significantly improved workplace health and safety environment。

According to OSHA， there are four elements of a successful injury in illness prevention program First。

 an organization's leadership must show commitment and employees must be encouraged to participate in workplace safety and health efforts。

Next， the organization must regularly engage in workplace hazard analyses。

Organizations also need to have a hazard prevention and control program in place to identify risk before it occurs。

Finally， organizations should hold regular safety training meetings so employees can learn about hazards and safe behaviors in the workplace。
