The final type of interview is the exit interview Exit interviews take place after employees have indicated they will be leaving an organization。

😊，The goal of an exit interview is to gain insight into why an employee has decided to leave。😊。

Retenenstion is critical to the success of any organization because high turnover rates are inefficient and costly。



![](img/33a718bacdb61bf23ccb29d2b9d24e20_16.png)

At Urban Attire， managers use exit interviews to identify reasons why employees are leaving and whether changes are needed to prevent future departures。

 for example， a series of exit interviews revealed that high performing employees are leaving because they feel there is greater potential for growth elsewhere with this feedback urban attire is considering extending more opportunities and leadership positions to at risk employees。
