 Let's explore employee referrals in more detail。 Many organizations have employee referral programs。

 referralral programs are systems or policies that incentivize current employees to recruit applicants for job openings。

 For example， a company might offer current employees a bonus for every candidate hired from their referral。

 There are great benefits to using employee referrals to source candidates for job openings。

 the main benefit of employee referrals is that they reduce the time and cost it takes to hire a new employee。

 Although a talent sourcing team should still be involved in the search。
