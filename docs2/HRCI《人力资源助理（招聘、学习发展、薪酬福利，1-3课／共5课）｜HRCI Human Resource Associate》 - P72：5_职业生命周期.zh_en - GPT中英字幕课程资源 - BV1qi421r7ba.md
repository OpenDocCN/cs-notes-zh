 Care life cycle refers to the stages that an individual goes through in their career from the beginning of their working life until their retirement。

 A career life cycle includes four stages。 Exp， establishment， maintenance and disengagement。

 Let's explore them further。😊。

In the exploration stage， an individual identifies the type of work they would like to pursue。

 they assess their values， interests， preferences， and available career opportunities。

If they are already working in a role， performance is typically low during this period as they focus on this exploration。

 the establishment stage is when an individual begins to create meaningful and relevant roles for themselves within the organization performance increases during this time because the employee is interested and engaged in their work。

The next stage is maintenance during the maintenance stage。

 an individual reaches a level within the organization where their talents are optimized。

 this is a plateau。At this stage， performance might be stagnant when your organization recognizes an employees in this phase。

 you can offer new challenges to reengage the employee these challenges might include new skills or mentoring coworkers。

Disengagement is a sage in which an employee pulls away from their work。

 Their priorities begin to shift， and they might even consider leaving the organization During this stage。

 performance is often low。 You can offset low performance with growth opportunities。

 such as a strong commitment to learning and development。
