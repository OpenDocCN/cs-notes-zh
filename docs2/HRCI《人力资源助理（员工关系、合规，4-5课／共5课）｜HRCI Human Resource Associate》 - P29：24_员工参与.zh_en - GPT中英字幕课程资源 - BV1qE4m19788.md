Employee involvement means employees have opportunities to get involved with how an organization functions and feel that their contributions make a difference。

An organization's leadership must be committed to listening to and considering the input of employees Let's explore some of the ways to help employees feel more involved。

 the task force disbands。😊，Another method is committees committees are groups that address ongoing issues within an organization。

 for example， some organizations might create an employee management committee that consists of individuals from different levels within the organization。
