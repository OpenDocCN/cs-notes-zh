The first type of incentive is a commission。 A commission is a percentage of a sale that is given to a worker for each product or service they sell。

 For example， a salesperson earns a 5% cent commission on each sale they make。

 These additional earnings based on their successful performance， increase their income。

 Another type of incentive is an annual bonus。 Workers who meet or exceed goals might receive an annual bonus。

 For example， if a worker completes a specific project by the end of the quarter。

 your organization might award them a bonus at the end of the year。

Stock options are a form of corporate compensation。

 Stock options allow workers to buy an organization's stock at a predetermined price。

 Stock options are an incentive because they encourage employees to consider an organization's long term success。

Your organization， for example， might offer stock options to employees if the organization performs well over time。

 the stock options value can increase， providing a financial reward to the worker。
