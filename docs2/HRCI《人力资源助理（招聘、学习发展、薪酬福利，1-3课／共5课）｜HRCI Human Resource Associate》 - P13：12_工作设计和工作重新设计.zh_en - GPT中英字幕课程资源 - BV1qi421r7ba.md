During the job design process， a human resources specialist identifies the duties， tasks。

There are a few tools you can use to design or redesign a job。 First。

 job simplification takes away a duty from one position and gives it to another。

 This is also known as job specialization。 Next， job enlargement increases a variety of tasks that are performed in a job to make it more meaningful。

 Finally， job enrichment attempts to add more stimulating and rewarding work into a particular job。😊。

Job enlargement and job enrichment both tend to involve the expansion of roles。

 This expansion can be thought of as either occurring horizontally or vertically。

 horizontalal expansion known as horizontal loading occurs when specialized jobs are replaced with more comprehensive positions。

Horizontal loading expands the number of tasks a worker is responsible for completing。

Vertical expansion， known as vertical loading， occurs when jobs are changed to give workers more responsibility and authority。

 Often， vertical loading shifts responsibilities from managers to workers themselves。 If need be。

 the human resources specialist， will organize the job。

 according to the human needs and motivations of the worker。

 rather than organizational Go for output and quality。 This is called the human method。😊。

Another approach is the job characteristics method when a human resources specialist conducts job design or job redesign。

 according to the job characteristics approach， it takes into account five dimensions of worker motivation。

 skill variety， task identity， task significance， autonomy and feedback。

Skill variety describes whether the duties in a job are routine or ever changing。

 Task identity is defined by the worker's involvement。

 whether it is from start to finish or the completion of a subtask。😊。

Task significance suggests the importance of a duty to the organization's success or to society。😊。

Autonomy relates to the freedom that an employee has to vary output。

Feedback is opinions on how the employee is performing on the job from managers or supervisors when a human resources specialist addresses all five motivations in the job characteristics method。

 the entire organization is highly motivated and operating at peak productivity。😊。

In a given job redesign effort for a particular job。

 human resources specialists should follow a series of proven procedures。First。

 reconcile any differences between what is in the job description or job specification with the actual work performed on the job Next。

 reassign duties， tasks and responsibilities to workers with more ability or motivation。😊。

Then offer training in the newly assigned responsibilities。Finally。

 revise the applicable job description and job specification and revisit regularly。

Designing and redesigning jobs has the potential to dramatically improve motivation and productivity throughout the whole organization。
