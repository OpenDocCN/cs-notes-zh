What if we want to summarize the customer table by creating two new columns to count how many customers are under 25 and how many are over 64 for each state？

We can use a Boolean expression to determine whether a customer is under or over a specific age。

Bullyan expressions are expressions that evaluate to one of two values， one， true， or zero false。



![](img/4e4453a4a79b14cfb5aeac3328b3a511_3.png)

For example， if age is less than 25， the value of under 25 is one true。If age is greater than 25。

 the value of under 25 is0 false。
