The knowledge skills and abilitiesbil or KSA Learn model was developed by the US federal government as a framework for hiring employees in this video you'll learn how to use this model to create training and development programs。



![](img/0fe539bef23226ea2b949d9e115ff256_2.png)

The KSA model is a framework used to define the knowledge。

 skills and abilities that an employee needs to be successful in a particular job。

 the KSA model is defined as knowledge， the theoretical understanding of concepts， skills。

 the application of knowledge through handson experience or training， and abilities。

 the innate talents or traits that an employee brings to the job。In HR。

 this model is used to define job requirements， evaluate candidates。

 and assess training and coaching needs for your current employees。



![](img/0fe539bef23226ea2b949d9e115ff256_4.png)
