Let's review some of the best practices for URLs。Don't change URLs for the sake of changing them。

If you do have to change a URL， always redirect that to a new page using a permanent 301 redirect。

It's always best to optimize the URL from the start。Incorporate keywords into the URL。

 where possible。Incorporate keywords into subdirecties， where possible。Keep URLs short and succinct。
