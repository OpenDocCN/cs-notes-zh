 Alex and HR has helped create this new training program that aligns with this initiative and wants to figure out how to evaluate its attractiveiveness and how it resonates with employees。

At first， Alex must determine how to measure the success of the training。

 Alex decides that those in the training should take three surveys。

 The surveys will ask the same questions before， right after and three weeks after the training to measure the effectiveness of the training。

 Since this is new training， the HR team decides to run a pilot program with a small group of employees from various departments to measure the program's reception。

The pilot group will provide their feedback through a survey that contains questions about content clarity。

 job relevance， effectiveness， and satisfaction。Survey results will give Alex insight on how to improve the training program before the organization wide launch For example。

 if employees find some content irrelevant， Alex can adjust the training to include more examples of how the new system can benefit each job role。

It is important for Alex that the new training is relevant to all members of the organization and running a pilot training program will help double check the content before everyone takes the training。

Alex knows keeping participants engaged can be challenging。

 so the survey includes questions about this too。Effective and engaging training is hard to target。

 and feedback from the pilot will really help。

To help participants feel appreciated after taking the survey。

 they will receive a $5 gift card from a local coffee shop。 It's a small thing to offer。

 But Alex hopes it will encourage people to complete the survey。😊。
