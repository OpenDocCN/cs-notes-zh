As you'll recall， HR professionals are required to collect important documents about employees throughout their time at an organization these documents are stored in an employee's personal employment file。

😊，An employer should create this file on the date the employee is hired。

An employee's personal file can be separated into four categories， employment records。

 payroll administration， performance management， and employee separation。

An employee's personal file can also include emergency contact information。

 details about child support and wage garnishments， and records of requests to review the file。



![](img/5f3ca7a414eada0cd609e49340c45d1a_4.png)

Employment records include necessary information about employees they include hiring paperwork such as job description。

 application and resume， they also include completed onboarding paperwork and a record of contracts between the employee and employer。

😊，The payroll administration category includes everything about an employee's payroll。

 it includes pay rate， tax forms and pay time off requests the third category is performance management。

😊，Documents in this category include performance evaluations， training history。

 letters of recognition， and written disciplinary notices the final category of HR documents is employee separation。

 This category consists of documents necessary for an employee's termination with an organization including a statement of resignation or layoff and a record of the final paycheck。
