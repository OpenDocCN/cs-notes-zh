Employee handbooks allow for more employee accountability with policies laid out in a go to reference。

 employees will always know what is expected from them。 With this in mind。

 it's important that an employee handbook be easy to find and access。

 It should either be printed out for all employees or a digital version should be easily accessible。

😊，Handbooks also allow for fluid conversations between employee and manager because of the equal access to the organization's policies。

The handbook itself is a growing and changing document。 As an organization grows and evolves。

 the handbook will， too。 new policies will need to be added。

 Not the organization from months or years in the past。😊。

One of the few problems that can arise from employee handbooks is when employers do not enforce the policies within the handbook。

 when policies are not enforced， employees will take the contents of the handbook less seriously。

 This is a major issue because a handbook should be the backbone of formalizing and systematizing policy within the organization。

Another issue that can arise is poorly written policies this can lead to an overall misunderstanding and employees cannot be as accountable for failure to conform to the handbook。
