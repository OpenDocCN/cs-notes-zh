Group incentivecent pay is a compensation system that rewards employees for achieving a collective goal。

 unlike individual incentive pay， this team based pay system is designed to motivate and encourage collaboration and teamwork。

With this pay system， a portion of the manager's compensation becomes at risk and is dependent on the team's achievement of specific goals。

 In other words， the manager's pay is linked to the team's success。

 It is a manager's responsibility to motivate the team to work together and achieve their collective goals。

 Group incentive pay is particularly suited for scenarios when is difficult to measure individual employee performance。

 It is used to reward achievement of a team goal， such as a sales target or timely project completion。

 profitfit sharing is a common form of group incentive pay。

 which you will learn about later in the lesson。Let's consider an example。

 the sales team for an online retailer has a sales target for this quarter。

 each team member will receive incentive pay if the group achieves the target。

The group incentive pay motivates the team members to work together， share responsibility。

 support each other and celebrate their collective success。

 This approach leads to better performance and results for the organization。😊。
