In other words， can we pick values for the voltage thresholds VOL， VIL， VIH， and VOH。

 so that the show in VTC meets the constraints imposed on a combinational device？



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_5.png)

An inverter outputs a digital one when its input is a digital zero and vice versa。



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_8.png)

In fact， this device does produce a high output voltage when it's input voltage to slow。

 so there's a chance this will work out。

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_10.png)

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_11.png)

The lowest output voltage produced by the device is 0。5 volts。

 so if the device is to produce a legal digital output of 0。

 we have to choose V OL to be at least to 0。5volts。



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_13.png)

We want the inverter to produce a valid digital0 whenever its input is a valid digital one。



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_15.png)

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_16.png)

Looking at the VTC， we see that if the input is higher than3 volts。

 the output will be less than or equal to VOL， so let's set VIH to3 volts。



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_18.png)

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_19.png)

We can set it to a higher value than  three volts， but we'll make it as low as possible to leave room for a generous。

 high noise margin。

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_21.png)

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_22.png)

That takes care of two of the four signal thresholds， VOL and VIH。



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_24.png)

The other two thresholds are related to these two by the noise margin N。

 as shown by these two equations。

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_26.png)

Can we find a value for n such that V out is greater than equal to VOH when V ins less than or equal to VIL？



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_28.png)

If we choose n as 0。5 volts， then the formulas tell us that VIL is1 volt and VOH is 3。5 volts。



![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_30.png)

Plotting these thresholds on the graph and adding the forbidden regions。

 we see that happily the VTC is， in fact， legal。

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_32.png)

![](img/eeb373ef6358701537da06ea6242d39b_33.png)

So we can use this device as a combinational inverter if we use the signaling specifications with VOL as half a volt。

 VIL is1 volt， VIH is 3 volts， and VOH is 3。5 volts。
