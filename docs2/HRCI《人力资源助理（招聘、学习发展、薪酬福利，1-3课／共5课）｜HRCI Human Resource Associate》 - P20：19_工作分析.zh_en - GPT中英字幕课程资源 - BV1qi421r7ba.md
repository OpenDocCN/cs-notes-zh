 job description and job specification。

![](img/92a76fb94fb8f06afbbc384a3a7dcb3e_2.png)

Job analysis is the study of the tasks and responsibilities of a job to determine the job's importance and its relation to other jobs in the company。

In order to hire the right person for a job， you have to know what skills and abilities the candidates need to have to be successful in the job。

 A job analysis takes stock of this by determining exactly what a specific job is responsible for。

The first component of a job analysis is the job description。

 A job description is a written document describing an employee's work tasks and responsibilities。

 This statement details a task performed on the job and the purpose of the job itself。😊。

When creating a job description， you include a job title。

 who the employee reports to and who reports to them， the key duties they will perform。

 and the agreed upon terms of employment for the job。



![](img/92a76fb94fb8f06afbbc384a3a7dcb3e_4.png)

In some cases， a job description might include personal characteristics that may be a bona fide occupational qualification。

 A bona fide occupational qualification， BFOQ is a condition that is reasonably necessary to conduct business operations。

 For example， Re institutions may make a person's religion a qualification criterion。

 or a swimsuit company may only hire female models for female swimsuits。

 Another example of this in practice， is that the federal aviation administration。

 FAAA has a minimum age for pilots and copis and does not hire individuals over the age of 60。😊。



![](img/92a76fb94fb8f06afbbc384a3a7dcb3e_6.png)

Deciding on these qualifications and their inclusion in job descriptions must be very carefully done in accordance with this Title V exception After identifying the job's main tasks and responsibilities。

 the next step is to create a job specification。😊，A job specification describes the qualifications and employee needs in order to perform a specific job。

The specification includes the role's required educational background and experience。

 any training relevant to the job， and the skills， knowledge and characteristics an applicant would need to be successful in the role。

😊。
