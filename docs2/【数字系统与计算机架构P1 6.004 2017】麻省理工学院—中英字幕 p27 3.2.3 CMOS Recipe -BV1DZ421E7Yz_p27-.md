When building pull down circuits that connect a signaling node to the ground rail of the power supply。

When the pull down circuit is conducting， the signaling node will be at zero volts and qualify as the digital value0。

If we obey this rule， NFs will act as switches controlled by VGS the difference between the voltage of the gate terminal and the voltage of the source terminal。

 When VGS is lower than the moss Fs threshold voltage， the switch is open or not conducting。

 and there is no connection between the moss Fts source and drain terminals。

If V Gs is greater than the threshold voltage， the switch is on or conducting。

 and there is a connection between the source and drain terminals。

 That path has a resistance determined by the magnitude of V Gs。The larger VgS。

 the lower the effective resistance of the switch， and the more current that will flow from drain to source。

When designing pull down circuits of NFt switches， we can use the following simple mental model for each NFt switch。

 If the gate voltage is a digital 0， the switch will be off。 If the gate voltage is a digital one。
