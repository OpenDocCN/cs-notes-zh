In this series of demonstrations， we're going to explore creating an Alice project。

There are two parts to creating an Alice project。The first is setting up the initial scene。

What 3D objects or models are going to be part of the animation we wish to create and where should those models be placed to start。

The second part is to actually compose the program to tell the story， making the animation happen。

The next few lectures will focus on the first part， namely setting up the initial scene。

In setting up a scene in Alice， you first need to decide what 3D models will be in your Alice project。

After they are added to the project， they will likely need to be positioned in the correct locations。

You can use either the mouse to move and orient the objects correctly。

 or you can use the one shot procedures to do so。
