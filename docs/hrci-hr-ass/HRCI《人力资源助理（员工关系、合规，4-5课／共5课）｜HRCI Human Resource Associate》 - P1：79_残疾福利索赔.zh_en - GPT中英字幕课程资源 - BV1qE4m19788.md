Disability benefits are usually provided by a private insurance company。

 disability benefits are typically described as short term STD or long term LTD。

 Insurance companies generally require documentation from the employee。

 Once documentation has been submitted， the insurance company will investigate the severity of the disability and either approve or deny disability benefits。

 Long term disability benefits can last between two and five years or until legal retirement age。

 These types of claims can encompass a number of different ailments。

 including neurological impairments like strokes or physical impairments like fractured vertebrae。
