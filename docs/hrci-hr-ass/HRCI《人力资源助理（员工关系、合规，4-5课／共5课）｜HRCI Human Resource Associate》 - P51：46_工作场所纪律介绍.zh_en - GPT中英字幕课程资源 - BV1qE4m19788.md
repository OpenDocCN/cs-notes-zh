Workplace discipline is a form of training that enforces an organization's rules。

 it can be used to modify behavior for problem employees or unproductive employees workplace discipline can get quite complicated。

 so a written document that outlines the organization's policies is essential。😊。

There are a few discipline methods that you should understand as an HR professional。

 the first is progressive discipline this approach includes steps that become sequentially more severe and are designed to change an employee's inappropriate behavior。



![](img/a3e5397abd99baf0c9cf2b493e289eb1_4.png)
