Learner engagement refers to the amount of time and effort learners put into the learning process。

There are a few ways to measure engagement。 One of the most common ways to measure learner engagement is through surveys。

 Surys can be administered before， during and after training to gauge the level of engagement in satisfaction of the learners。

Questions can be asked about the relevance and usefulness of the training material。

 the quality of the delivery and the overall experience of the training。

Feedback forms can also be used to gather feedback on specific training sessions or modules feedback forms can be used to gather information on the content。

 delivery and relevance of the training material。Observing learners during the training can provide insights into their engagement levels。

 observers can take notes on learner behavior such as their level of participation， attentiveness。

 and interactions with the training material and other learners。

Performance data can also be used to measure the effectiveness of training。

Performance data can include test scores， job performance。

 and other metrics that can be used to measure the impact of the training on the learners。

Let's explore an example， a global software organization Connective。

 recently implemented a sales training program for its employees to enhance their skills and knowledge in selling the organization's products。

The organization wanted to evaluate the effectiveness of the program and measure the level of learner engagement to make improvements to the program in the future。

The Human resources team determined the level of engagement through surveys。

 feedback forms and observations。Surveys gathered pre and post training provide feedback on relevance。

 usefulness， delivery quality， and overall experience on specific training sessions or modules。

Observers noted participation， attentiveness， and interaction with material and peers。



![](img/ede42de531c0e5e82db5e54a18319985_7.png)

The results of this survey show that 89% of the learners found that the training material was relevant and useful。

 and 93% rated the quality of delivery as， excellent or good。

The feedback forms showed that the learners were particularly engaged in the role playing exercises。

 which allowed them to practice their selling skills in a simulated environment。

 The observers noted that the learners were active participants in the training。

 asking questions and engaging in discussions with the other trainers and other learners。

Based on the results of the learner engagement measures and the performance data。

 the connected HR team concluded that the training program was effective in enhancing the learner' skills and knowledge in selling the organization's products。

 they also identify opportunities to improve the program further by enhancing the engagement levels of the learners in some specific modules learner engagement can be very useful in measuring the training cost per employee because it helps gauge whether the financial cost was worth it in improving employee knowledge and development。
