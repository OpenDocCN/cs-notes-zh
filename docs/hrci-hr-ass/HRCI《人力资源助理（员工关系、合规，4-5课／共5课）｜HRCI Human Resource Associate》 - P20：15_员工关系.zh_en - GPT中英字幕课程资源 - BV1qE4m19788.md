When an employer hires a new employee， they are not just bringing a new member into an organization。

 but also starting a new relationship。Managing employee relationships is vital for business success。

 solid relationships foster employee happiness and enhance productivity。😊。

 HR professionals should keep the dynamics of an organization's employer employee relationship in mind Let's explore these relationships。

![](img/3ce8023078701f9e81c73c6a4383873b_2.png)

According to the International Labor Organization， an employment relationship is the legal link between employers and employees。

 it exists when a person performs work or services under certain conditions and return for remuneration or pay。

There are various relationships between employers and employees with three common categories。

 employees， contractors， and freelancers。

![](img/3ce8023078701f9e81c73c6a4383873b_4.png)

Employees work directly for an organization under a contract of employment。

They typically have a long term commitment to the organization。

 receipt benefits and are subject to rights and responsibilities defined by labor laws。

Employees often contribute to an organization's day to day operations are an integral part of its workforce。

 Freelancers are self employedloed individuals who provide services to multiple clients。

 They often work on a project by project basis and have more autonomy in managing their work。😊。

They can choose the projects they work on。Contractors also provide services to organizations。

 however they differ from freelancers。Contractors typically work on a specific project or a fixed term basis。

 they may have their own business or work for a contracting agency and fulfill a particular need within an organization。

😊，The nature of a relationship can significantly influence an employee's commitment to an organization's mission and overall goals。

Employees with a direct employment relationship experience a deeper connection to an organization and its mission they may understand an organization's goals and objectives leading to increased commitment and engagement。

😊。

![](img/3ce8023078701f9e81c73c6a4383873b_6.png)

On the other hand， contractors and freelancers may be less attached to an organization's mission and vision。

Their primary focus is typically to deliver a specific project or service。

 and their commitment may be more task oriented rather than driven by a long term investment in an organization's goals。



![](img/3ce8023078701f9e81c73c6a4383873b_8.png)

At will employment allows employers and employees to terminate the employment relationship without cause。

 offering flexibility in employment decisions。Rightrite to work laws focus on the issue of union membership and dues these laws give employees a choice to join or support a union without havinging to pay dues。

Both concepts contribute to a dynamic and adaptable employment and labor practices framework。
