What is broadbanding Brobanding is an approach to structuring pay it creates a limited number of salary bands with a very large difference between the minimum and maximum pay levels。

When organizations use broadbanding， all jobs fall into one of the pay levels， for example。

 an organization might combine all sales related rules into a single sales band。Within this ban。

 there are different salary ranges or levels。 The levels for the sales band are 30000 to 40000 for entry level employees。

45000 to 60000 for mid level employees and 70000 to 80000 for highly skilled sales professionals。

Broadbending creates a sense of fairness regarding compensation and limits the perception that some workers earn more than others for similar types of work。

Hr professionals can also use the green and red circle technique to structure pay。

 This technique determines whether workers' compensation falls within the range for workers with similar skills。

 experience and responsibilities。 you've learned about this technique earlier in this course。

 As a reminder， workers earning below the minimum compensation range are in the green circle。

 When workers earn above the maximum compensation range， they are in the red circle。

Job evaluation is also an important part of the pay structure process。

 All jobs at an organization should be categorized according to the level of responsibility and skills required。

Through job evaluation， HR professionals determine the appropriate pay range for each position based on experience。

 education and responsibilities This process helps ensure workers are paid fairly for their work and their contributions within an organization。
