You have learned a lot about various appraisal methods and how important they are to an employee and organization's success。

 Today， we will discuss feedback and the appraisal process。 As you'll recall。

 an employee's performance is often reviewed by an employee's supervisor。 However。

 it's becoming increasingly common to collect performance measures not only from superiors。

 but peers and subordinates as well。 This is known as 360 degree feedback。

360 degree feedback includes all levels of workers who interact with the employee。

 This approach provides more detailed performance information than a supervisor only approach。

360 degree feedback also ensures that employees do not mistreat their peers or subordinates and act respectfully to their superiors。

😊。

![](img/eb921c3485e95c3905f799e6430e665a_2.png)

360 degree feedback encourages employees to respond appropriately and non defensively in feedback situations。

 in fact， how employees respond to feedback is just as important as a feedback content。
