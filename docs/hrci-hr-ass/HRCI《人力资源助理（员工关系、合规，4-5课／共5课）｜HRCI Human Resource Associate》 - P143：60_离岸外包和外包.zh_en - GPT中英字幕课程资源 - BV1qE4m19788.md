Outsourcing is a practice of paying an outside entity to perform specialized business functions。

These functions are generally outside of an organization's core competencies。

 therefore it's a beneficial to hire another entity to maintain that function for them。😊。

A core competency is a specific function that an organization regards as central to their success。😊。



![](img/12096be70a6ff03004790d819d9e6491_4.png)

A few common examples of outsourcing include security， food service， or payroll for an organization。

The next business strategy an HR professional needs to be familiar with is offshoring。

Offshoring is when an organization establishes a foreign subsidiary and typically shifts work from higher paid American workers to less expensive workers outside the US。



![](img/12096be70a6ff03004790d819d9e6491_8.png)

Offshoring is a popular concept when organizations need to handle large， labor intensive functions。

Most frequently， these functions include customer service。

 technical support and computer programming for example。
