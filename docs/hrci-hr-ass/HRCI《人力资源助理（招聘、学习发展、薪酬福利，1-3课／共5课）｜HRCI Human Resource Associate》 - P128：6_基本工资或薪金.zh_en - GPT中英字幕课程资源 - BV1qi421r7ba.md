A standard base rate or salary program offers a fixed monetary reward for performing standard duties。

 This amount does not include benefits， bonuses or commissions。

 There are designated minimum and maximum levels within those pay scales to account for variations in experience and skill levels。

As this is competency based pay， it often does not recognize or encourage any learning that occurs。

Merriit pay is a variation on the base salary that compensates employees through their salary range for their position based on their performance。

The pay is reviewed in concurrence with the annual performance reviews。

 One problem with this arrangement is that some employees will come to expect that they will routinely get a raise after each evaluation。

 eliminating the motivational impact of the extra pay。

 this is an increase in base wage or salary meant to compensate for the falling value of the dollar due to inflation。

 leaving employees with the same buying power for their pay。
