There are two main steps to creating an animation。First， you need to design the animation。

 you need to plan each scene of the animation， including the location of each object。

This can be done with a storyboard。Once you have designed the animation。

 then you are ready to translate your design into code。

The more time and depth that you put into the design。

 the less time you will spend in debugging the code。So what is a storyboard？

It is a series of simple sketches that show what happens in each scene of the animation。

You don't need to be an artist。 Stick people are fine to use as long as you can tell which way they are facing。

It is important to have a list of all the objects that will be in each scene of the animation and to know the location of each object and its spatial relationship to other objects。

It's also important to include a description of what you want to happen in each scene。

Why should you create a storyboard？ Well， in the real world。

 movie companies like Pixar create storyboards for their movies。

 The writer writes the story and then gives the story to an artist who sketches all the scenes and then pens the scenes up in order on a wall。

 Then the moment of truth comes。 The writer and the artist bring the story to life by describing the story to the director through the sketches and their words。

 The story pitch is very important as a director will decide if the movie should be produced。😊。

 an alien and a rock for the alien to hide behind。Note that our spaceship needs to have a door or doors that open and close。
