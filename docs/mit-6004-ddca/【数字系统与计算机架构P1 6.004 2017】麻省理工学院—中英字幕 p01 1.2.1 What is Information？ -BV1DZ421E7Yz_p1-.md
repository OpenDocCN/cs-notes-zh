Let's start by asking， what is information？From our engineering perspective。

 we'll define information as data communicated or received that resolves uncertainty about a particular factor or circumstance。

 In other words， after receiving the data， we'll know more about that particular factor circumstance。

The greater the uncertainty resolved by the data， the more information the data has conveyed。
