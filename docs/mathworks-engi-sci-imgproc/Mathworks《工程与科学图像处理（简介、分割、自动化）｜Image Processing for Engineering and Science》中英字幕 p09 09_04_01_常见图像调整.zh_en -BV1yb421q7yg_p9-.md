In this module， you'll learn how to visualize and adjust an image's contrast。

 changing the individual pixel values to provide more uniform illumination throughout the image。



![](img/76b851b5c336d3508383a76b601896e1_9.png)

Image contrast refers to the difference between the dark and bright areas of an image。



![](img/76b851b5c336d3508383a76b601896e1_12.png)

An image histogram helps visualize the distribution of pixel brightness values。

After inspecting the image histogram， you'll change the values of individual pixels by stretching out the histogram to use the full range。



![](img/76b851b5c336d3508383a76b601896e1_14.png)

Or by reshaping the histogram to bring out specific details of the image。



![](img/76b851b5c336d3508383a76b601896e1_16.png)
